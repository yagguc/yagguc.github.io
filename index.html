Yağmur Güçlütürk

human perception machine perception neurotechnology

Hello, My name is Yağmur. I am a postdoctoral researcher at Radboud University, Donders Institute for Brain, Cognition and Behaviour, Nijmegen, Netherlands. Welcome to my academic personal website!
I am interested in developing intelligent algorithms for the emerging assistive technologies that either interface or interact with people to improve their lives in health and disease, and do research in human perception, machine perception and neurotechnology. I did my Ph.D. and M.Sc. (with cum laude) in cognitive neuroscience at RU, and B.IT (with firsts and book award) in artificial intelligence at MMU. My present project is a part of the Neuronal Stimulation for Recovery of Function Consortium, and my past projects were funded by the personal grants that I received from Netherlands Universities Foundation for International Cooperation and Royal Netherlands Academy of Arts and Sciences. In my free time, I do indie app and game development, and hope to have my first release soon!
